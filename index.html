Achievements

Recognizing excellence from our students and our college.

Student Hackathon Winners

Our innovative student teams secured first place in multiple state-level and national hackathons, showcasing their problem-solving and tech skills.

Several teams received special mentions for innovation and social impact in technology-based challenges.

Students have also represented the college in several Hackathon, developing real-time solutions for local government and educational institutions.

Research Publications

Faculty and students have published numerous papers in high-impact academic journals, contributing to business and management knowledge.

Topics include Artificial Intelligence in Business, Sustainable Management Models, and FinTech Innovations.

Faculty guides have also received Best Research Paper Awards for excellence in entrepreneurship studies..

Sports Championships

Proud winners of several intercollegiate sports tournaments, demonstrating excellence in athletics and team spirit.

Community Initiatives

Our students lead impactful outreach programs that benefit local communities through sustainability, education, and social welfare activities.

Collaborated with NGOs for “Young Entrepreneurs for Change”, helping small entrepreneurs with marketing and finance mentoring.
